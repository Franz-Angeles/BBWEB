Hi, I'm Franz & I'm a Fitness Coach

I'm a 20 years old Men's Physique Bodybuilding athlete. A normal teenager that fell into the passion of bodybuilding because of my elder brothers, who already started this career line when I was a kid, and got inspired by them and had my first men's physique competition debut in July 2023 at KG Classic, Trained hard, became addicted to the progress, love the pain embrace the gains, fear of being average.

Like many others, I grew up as a skinny, lightweight boy who frequently played video games. pandemic hit us, there's no place to go but home. My eldest brother, who used to be the first bodybuilder in our family, bought a lot of gym equipment so he can continue his exercise at home. That was the time I was introduced to this hobby/sport.

Making this project/website is intended for my online fitness coaching, dedicated to helping individuals to transform their bodies and build lasting, healthy habits no matter where they are in the world. My approach combines personalized training, sustainable nutrition guidance, and real accountability to help you reach your goals. Online coaching is not just about focusing on the right diet and the right exercises to execute, but also the friendship that builds out.
